PrepPal – Meal Planning & Grocery Shopping App

Process: I designed PrepPal to provide a seamless meal planning experience by integrating personalized meal recommendations, grocery list management, and step-by-step cooking guidance. The project involved conducting user research, competitor analysis, and iterative prototyping to create a user-centric solution that simplifies meal prep while reducing food waste.

Skills/Techniques: User research, competitor analysis, wireframing, prototyping, UI design, usability testing, and visual branding using Figma.
