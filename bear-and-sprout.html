Bear & Sprout – Accessible Shopping for Children’s Products

I designed Bear & Sprout, an intuitive e-commerce platform that simplifies shopping for children's products by improving navigation, filtering options, and personalization. Through user research, wireframing, and prototyping, I created a seamless, user-friendly experience that helps parents and caregivers find age-appropriate products quickly and efficiently.
