About Me

My Story

Hi, I'm Nadia Somani, a UX/UI designer with a unique blend of experience in user research, data-driven design, and product strategy. With a background in public health and extensive experience leading cross-functional teams in healthcare, financial services, and social impact sectors, I bring a deep understanding of human behavior and empathy-driven design to every project. My approach combines rigorous qualitative and quantitative research with creative problem-solving to craft intuitive and engaging digital experiences that meet both user needs and business goals.

I have a strong passion for turning complex challenges into elegant, user-centered solutions. Whether it’s designing intuitive interfaces, optimizing workflows, or conducting in-depth usability testing, I thrive in environments where I can leverage research insights to inform strategic design decisions. My experience spans across web and mobile platforms, ensuring accessibility, inclusivity, and usability are at the forefront of my design process.

What makes me unique is my ability to bridge the gap between data and design, translating analytical insights into compelling user experiences. My diverse background in public health research has honed my ability to approach problems holistically, ensuring that every design decision considers the broader impact on user well-being, accessibility, and inclusion.

Beyond design, I'm deeply interested in human psychology, behavioral economics, and how technology can empower people to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives. I’m continuously expanding my skill set to stay at the forefront of emerging UX/UI trends and best practices.
